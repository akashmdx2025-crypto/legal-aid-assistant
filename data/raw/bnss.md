# Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita (BNSS) 2023 - Key Extracts

## Section 173: Information in Cognizable Cases (FIR)
Every information relating to the commission of a cognizable offence, if given orally to an officer in charge of a police station, shall be reduced to writing by him or under his direction.
**Digital FIR**: Information may also be given by electronic communication, and it shall be taken on record on being signed within three days by the person giving it.

## Section 35: Arrest how made
In making an arrest, the police officer or other person making the same shall actually touch or confine the body of the person to be arrested, unless there be a submission to the custody by word or action.
**Rights upon Arrest**: The person arrested has the right to be informed of the grounds of arrest and the right to bail if the offence is bailable (Section 47).

## Section 176: Recording of confessions and statements
Any Magistrate may, whether or not he has jurisdiction in the case, record any confession or statement made to him in the course of an investigation.
**Audio-Video Recording**: The process of recording search and seizure shall be recorded through audio-video electronic means (Section 105).
