# Consumer Protection Act 2019 - Key Extracts

## Section 2: Rights of Consumers
Consumers have the right to be protected against the marketing of goods, products or services which are hazardous to life and property.

## Section 2(9): Right to be Informed
The right to be informed about the quality, quantity, potency, purity, standard and price of goods, products or services, as the case may be, so as to protect the consumer against unfair trade practices.

## Section 58: Jurisdiction of District Commission
The District Commission shall have jurisdiction to entertain complaints where the value of the goods or services paid as consideration does not exceed one crore rupees.

## Section 94: Prevention of Unfair Trade Practices in E-commerce
The Central Government may, for the purposes of preventing unfair trade practices in e-commerce, direct selling and also to protect the interests and rights of consumers, prescribe such measures as it may consider necessary.
